Esmeta Control Tower

Esmeta Control Tower, is a bot designed to test:

  • SAZLR (Single Action Zamor Launching Robot) System
  • Ultrasonic Sensor

ECT is my first bot using NXT. I have started to program with NXT-G. At present, I am testing Icommand 0.5 & NXT#
