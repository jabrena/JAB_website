NXT Gyroscope

NXT Gyroscope is a evolution of my first design to build ECT. I saw 2 projects in NXTLOG: Carbot and Guardturret and I decided to improve ECT's phisical design.

My first improvement was to have NXT brick over the platform in ECT then I have to design a new gyroscope. I saw that projects and I had a new idea.

If you use my new design you will notice that the engineering desing is very robuts, because you will not have any vibration.
