Projects

Since 2005, I have invested hundreds of hours in developing a tools to improve the control of sales & customers.

Now, I am developing a web application to control the sales of any company. The goals to reach are:

  • The application should connect to any ERP using ODBC technology
  • The application should manage SQL & MDX
  • The application should help to Marketing Managers analyzing Sales, Customers, Locations & Products
  • The application should work alone

The features of my projects are

  • Connectivity with ERP systems using ODBC
  • Integration with R & MONDRIAN
  • Sales budget control
  • Sales prediction
  • Life cycle Analisis
  • Export to Excel

Every Marketing Manager should have excellent Sale & Customer Reports to make correct decissions.
A lot of people use Excel and others tools to do his reports, but if they could use the power of ERP's BD then, they would improve the results.

I like to know the tendence of sales and the health of the profits, but when you have a global view of the sales then you can make good decissions. The tool that I am developing can analyze the sales's customer to know the best way to sell more.

I often make several questions about:

  • Global vision of the sales and profits
  • Sales prediction
  • Customer's clusters
  • Sales tendences to make campaing to increase the sales
